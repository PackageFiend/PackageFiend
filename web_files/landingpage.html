About the Project
Our Inspiration:
Before this project began, a member of our team was building his first computer. Each day, in his excitement, he found himself going through emails, jumping from website to website to get updates on the whereabouts of all the parts for the project. It was time-consuming, but he was excited for all the orders to arrive. The idea of having a unified platform, one place to bring all the tracking together, was our inspiration for this project.
Holberton School:
The project was built as a portfolio project to finish our first academic year at Holberton School in SF. It was planned, designed, and coded from backend, frontend, deployment, over 4 weeks. Due to COVID-19, all work was completed remotely.
We are studying a full-stack curriculum, and intend to become well-rounded and versatile software engineers. Feedback is appreciated!
